Plushies For Life

Plushies for Life was established on 19 March 2010. Our headquarters and factory is located at Violy Road and Violy Drive, in the West region of Singapore. They opened on 21 March 2010 by CEO Tan Yu Kang.

Our nearest MRT station is Violy MRT Station, along the Andrew Orozco MRT Line. The nearby bus stops are 'Opp Violy Stn' and 'Violy Stn', which is located along Violy Circle and served by Bus Services 88, 133, 190, 234 and 981. It is just a 5-minute walk. We are also located within a 3-minute walk to Gustav Food Center, where you can get food and drinks at affordable prices!

We manufacture and sell plushies to customers of all ages, ranging from types like dinosaurs, wild animals, comic book heroes and transportation. As of 29 March 2025, we have over 450 employees, working in fields like manufacturing to selling as well as customer enquiries. As of 12 January 2025, we have made over $390,000 in revenue. Our plushies can also be found in over 40 stores countrywide.

On 10 November 2014, we manufactured and gave out over 600 plushies to attendees who attended the 'Children's Fun Event', located at Dallas Mall in Jakir Road.

In August 2015, as part of SG50 celebrations, we manufactured and sold over 750 plushie bears to members of the public. The bears wore SG50 on their t-shirts.

This website is where you can purchase your favourite plushies at affordable prices at the comfort of your home without the need to go to physical stores. This will save your travelling and ordering time.
